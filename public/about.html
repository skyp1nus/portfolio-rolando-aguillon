ABOUT

Endless esthetique

In 2015, Rolando began to explore the world of digital design, for the next two years, the main point of efforts was a graphic design, branding identity, posters for music albums and films by independent directors. But the real growth happened for him in profession of user interface designer for games, websites and applications. It's a very valuable experience to helping entrepreneurs create great products for their audience.

In 2023, Rolando decides to take the next step in his career path, by creating his own game named “Artman”, where he embodies a deep passion for fantasy worlds, where a human is just a small adventurer in an infinite universe.
